Coach Hicham Hakam

Coach Hicham Hakam is recognized as the first Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) black belt in Morocco and North Africa. With a deep passion for martial arts, he has dedicated his life to teaching and promoting BJJ across the region. His coaching philosophy emphasizes discipline, confidence, and personal growth, making him a respected figure in the BJJ community.
